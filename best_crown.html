    Frequently Asked Questions

    You May Ask

    What are dental crowns?
    Dental crowns are caps placed over damaged or weakened teeth to restore their shape, size, and strength.
    What is a dental bridge?
    A dental bridge is a prosthetic device used to replace one or more missing teeth by anchoring to adjacent teeth or implants.
    How long does a crown or bridge last?
    With proper care, dental crowns and bridges can last between 10 to 15 years or longer.
    Are crowns and bridges painful?
    The procedure is generally not painful, though you may experience some discomfort or sensitivity during the recovery period.
    How do I care for my crowns and bridges?
    Maintain good oral hygiene by brushing and flossing regularly, and visit your dentist for routine check-ups.
    How much do crowns and bridges cost?
    The cost varies based on the complexity and materials used. We provide affordable options and transparent pricing.
    Can I eat normally with crowns and bridges?
    Yes, you can eat normally, but it's advisable to avoid very hard or sticky foods to ensure longevity.
    Are there any risks involved with crown and bridge treatments?
    Risks are minimal but may include temporary sensitivity or discomfort. Follow post-treatment care to minimize risks.
    Why should I choose Smile Works Dental Care for crowns and bridges?
    We offer expert care, affordable solutions, and personalized attention to ensure the best results for your dental needs.
    How long does the procedure take?
    The entire process typically involves multiple visits over a few weeks, depending on the complexity of the case.
