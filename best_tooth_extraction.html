    Process of Tooth Extraction in Hyderabad

    At Smile Works Dental Care, the tooth extraction process is handled with precision and care. First, Our specialist will perform a thorough examination, including X-rays if needed, to assess the tooth’s condition. Next, the area around the tooth will be numbed with a local anesthetic to ensure a painless experience.

    Once you are comfortable, the tooth will be carefully removed using specialized tools. In cases of complex extractions, such as wisdom teeth, additional steps may be required to ensure a smooth procedure. Post-extraction care instructions will be provided to aid in your recovery and ensure optimal healing.

    Any Side Effects for Tooth Extraction

    While tooth extraction is generally safe, some side effects can occur. Common effects include mild discomfort, swelling, and minor bleeding, which usually subside within a few days. It’s important to follow the post-extraction care instructions provided by Dr. Siddhartha Puppala to minimize these effects and promote healing.

    In rare cases, more significant side effects such as infection, dry socket or parasthesia may occur. If you experience severe pain, prolonged bleeding, or any unusual symptoms, contact us immediately for guidance and further care.

    Aftercare for Tooth Extraction in Hyderabad

    Post-extraction medication is often prescribed to manage pain and prevent infection. Our specialist may recommend over-the-counter pain relievers or prescribe antibiotics if necessary. It’s crucial to take the medication as directed and adhere to any additional recommendations to ensure a smooth recovery.

    Our team will provide detailed instructions on how to manage discomfort and care for the extraction site. If you have any concerns about the medication or experience any adverse reactions, please reach out to us promptly.
